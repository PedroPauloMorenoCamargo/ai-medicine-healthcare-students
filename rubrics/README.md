# Grading Rubrics

This directory contains the grading rubrics for all course components.

## Assessment Overview

| Component | Weight | Details |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Group Labs** (8×) | 30% | Weekly exercises (Weeks 2-9), 3.75% each |
| **Midterm Project** | 15% | Week 8 group project |
| **Final Project** | 40% | Weeks 10-14 capstone project |
| **Individual Participation** | 15% | Attendance, engagement, peer feedback |

**Total:** 100%

### Individual Grade Adjustments
- Base grade from group submission
- Individual grades may vary ±10% based on peer evaluations
- Consistent low contribution results in grade reduction

## Grading Philosophy

- **Learning Process Valued:** Partial credit for well-reasoned attempts
- **Documentation Matters:** Explain your thinking
- **Code Quality Counts:** Readable, organized, reproducible
- **Clinical Relevance:** Connect to real medical applications

## Late Submission Policy

- **0-24 hours late:** 10% penalty
- **24-48 hours late:** 25% penalty
- **>48 hours late:** Case-by-case (contact instructor)
- **Documented emergencies:** Extensions available

## Academic Integrity

All work must be your own or your group's work. You may:
- ✅ Discuss concepts with classmates
- ✅ Search for help online (cite sources!)
- ✅ Use AI tools for understanding concepts
- ✅ Collaborate within your assigned group

You may NOT:
- ❌ Copy code from other groups
- ❌ Submit others' work as your own
- ❌ Share assignment solutions publicly
- ❌ Use solutions from previous years

**Penalties:** Range from zero on assignment to course failure.

## Questions About Grading?

- **During grading:** Ask TAs for clarification
- **After grading:** Can dispute within 1 week
- **Policy questions:** Contact instructor
- **Rubric questions:** Post on discussion board
